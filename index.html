Regulation Watch

GDPR — EU General Data Protection Regulation Active

Enforcement actions continue to rise. Recent fines from EDPB member countries signal tightening scrutiny on AI training data and cross-border transfers.

↳ Latest: EDPB Guidelines on AI and data processing released Q1 2025
DPDP Act — India Digital Personal Data Protection Rules Pending

The DPDP Rules 2025 are under consultation. Key provisions around data fiduciary obligations, consent management, and the Data Protection Board remain under active drafting.

↳ Status: Draft Rules published; final notification awaited
EU AI Act In Force

Risk classification requirements for AI systems now create direct intersections with data governance programs — especially around training data provenance and documentation.

↳ High-risk system obligations apply from Aug 2026
US State Privacy Laws (CCPA / CPRA + Patchwork) Active

19+ US states now have active privacy laws. Data governance teams face a complex patchwork of opt-out, consent, and deletion obligations across jurisdictions.

↳ Texas, Montana, Oregon laws effective 2024–2025
